Controls:
W/S - Change Animation
Space - Replay Animation

Arrow Keys - Manipulate Offsets
Ctrl + Click - Manipulate Offsets with Mouse
    (or Click with Drag Move Enabled)

T - Change Current Character
Shift + Click - Change to Hovered-over Character

G - Flip Current Character

J/K/I/L - Move Camera
Middle Mouse - Move Camera with Mouse

F - Move Camera to Current Character's Follow Cam
Shift + F - Move Camera to Current Character's Midpoint

E/Q - Increase/Decrease Zoom
Scroll Wheel - Increase/Decrease Zoom
